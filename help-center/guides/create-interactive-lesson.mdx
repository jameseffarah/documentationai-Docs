## Overview

Build and publish your first interactive lesson for your class.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a lesson">
    Go to your dashboard, click **New Lesson**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add content">
    Use the editor to add text, images, or videos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add gamified activities">
    Insert a quiz or drag-and-drop activity from the toolbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** to make the lesson available to your students.
  </Step>
</Steps>
