---
title: Cannot Access Lesson
description: Quick fix for students unable to access lessons.
---

## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" message
- Lesson page does not load

## Causes

- Student not enrolled
- Internet connectivity issue

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check enrollment">
    Ensure the student has accepted the invite from their email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh connection">
    Ask the student to refresh the browser, or try logging out and in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If the issue remains, reach out to support@llamaroo.com.
  </Step>
</Steps>
