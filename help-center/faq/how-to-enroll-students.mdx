## Overview

Enroll students to unlock interactive lessons in your course.

## Steps

1. Go to your Llamaroo dashboard
2. Select your course
3. Click **Students** tab
4. Click **Invite Students**
5. Enter email addresses or generate an invite link
6. Students receive an email to join

<Callout kind="tip">
Students need to accept the invitation to access the course.
</Callout>
